About Me

GIS Specialist & Forest Resource Management Expert

Professional Biography

As a dedicated GIS specialist and forest resource management expert, I bring together technical expertise and environmental passion to create sustainable solutions for forest conservation and management challenges.

With over a decade of experience in the field, I've worked on projects ranging from national forest inventories to real-time deforestation monitoring systems. My approach combines advanced spatial technologies with a deep understanding of ecological processes to deliver actionable insights for decision-makers.

I'm committed to promoting the use of open-source technologies and making spatial data more accessible and usable for environmental management. Through my work, I aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical field applications.
